Welcome To Online Blood Donation System

A voluntary blood donors platform.

Every Year about 5 crore units of blood is required in our country. Out of which, only 2.5 crore units of blood are available.

Online Blood Donation System is an online platform where donors and recipients or patients could meet together.

Our blood donors donate blood for emergency need, the needy and natural calamities.
